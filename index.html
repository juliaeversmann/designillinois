design illinois career fair

when & where

friday, march 22 9:30-3:00 pm knoll showroom at fulton market chicago, illinois

about

the university of illinois urbana-champaign is proud to take part in the 2024 design illinois career and networking fair. this event offers graduating and career-ready students the opportunity to interview for jobs and network with companies. students in graphic design, sustainable design, and industrial designs programs will be attending.
