Design Illinois Career Fair

When & Where

Friday, March 22 9:30-3:00 pm Knoll Showroom at Fulton Market Chicago, Illinois

About

The University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign is proud to take part in the 2024 Design Illinois Career and Networking Fair. This event offers graduating and career-ready students the opportunity to interview for jobs and network with companies. Students in graphic design, sustainable design, and industrial design programs will be attending.
